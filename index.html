Phaneroo ministries also does activities ins the society is to say feeding street childen, clothing them,and educating them.Recently the ministry also participated in helping the kitezi slum victims by consoling the bereaved families ,giving matresses,clothes and food.

Phaneroo Definition is to make manifest or visible or known what has to be hidden or unknown.To manifest whether by words or deeds or any other way.To make actual and visible, realised known by teaching.
